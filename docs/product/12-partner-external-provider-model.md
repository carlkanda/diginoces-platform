# 12 — Partner and External Provider Model

## 1. Purpose

This document defines how external planners and providers participate in the Diginoces platform.

---

## 2. Business role

External planners/providers are partners who bring weddings to Diginoces.

They operate under Diginoces brand, pricing, contracts, and service standards.

They are not independent white-label tenants in version 1.

---

## 3. Partner permissions

Partners can:

- create draft wedding projects;
- submit projects for Diginoces approval;
- manage assigned projects operationally;
- communicate with the couple in project comments;
- view project progress where assigned.

Partners cannot:

- manage Diginoces pricing;
- see revenue;
- approve payment exceptions;
- manage contracts/templates;
- view internal notes;
- view audit logs;
- manage commissions.

---

## 4. Project creation workflow

1. Partner creates project draft.
2. Partner fills project/couple/event basics.
3. Partner submits project for review.
4. Diginoces/admin reviews and approves/rejects.
5. Once approved, the system can generate the contract.
6. Couple access opens only after contract approval.

---

## 5. Partner tracking

The system should track which projects were brought by each partner.

Track:

- partner ID;
- project ID;
- project status;
- source notes;
- project count;
- operational role.

Revenue remains visible only to Diginoces/admin.

No commission management is included in version 1.

---

## 6. Communication

Partners may communicate directly with the couple through a simple project comment/message thread.

Diginoces/admin should be able to see these communications.

Sensitive pricing, payment, and contract discussions remain controlled by Diginoces/admin.

---

## 7. Summary

The partner model allows Diginoces to scale through external planners while retaining control over brand, pricing, contracts, payments, and service quality.
